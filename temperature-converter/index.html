Conversion Formulas

  • °C to °F: (°C × 9/5) + 32
  • °F to °C: (°F − 32) × 5/9
  • °C to K: °C + 273.15
  • K to °C: K − 273.15
  • °F to K: (°F − 32) × 5/9 + 273.15
  • K to °F: (K − 273.15) × 9/5 + 32
